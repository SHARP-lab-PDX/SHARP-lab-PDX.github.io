Meet the team

Sleep research is a multidisciplinary field, at the crossroads of neuroscience, biology, physiology, medicine, mental health and wellbeing, and even philosophy.

Our team members hail from diverse backgrounds including:

    Scientists – neuroscientists, physiologists, physicists, computer scientists, systems scientists
    Clinicians – neurologists, psychologists, nurses, personal trainers, health coaches
    Engineers – electrical and mechanical engineers, materials scientists
    Students – undergraduate, masters, and PhD graduate students
